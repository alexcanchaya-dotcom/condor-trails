Your Journey

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Ten days of discovery through Peru's most iconic landscapes, from coastal Lima to the sacred heights of the Andes.

1

Arrive Lima

Lima

Welcome to Peru. Settle into your Miraflores hotel, then join us for a memorable welcome dinner at one of Lima's top cevicherías. Stroll the Miraflores district as the Pacific sun sets over the cliffs.

2

Lima — Culture & Cuisine

Lima

Explore the world-renowned Larco Museum and its pre-Columbian collection, then wander through the bohemian quarter of Barranco with its street art and colonial charm. Finish the day with a hands-on Peruvian cooking class.

3

Fly to Cusco

Cusco

A short morning flight over the Andes brings you to the ancient Inca capital. Take the afternoon to acclimatise at altitude, visit the vibrant San Pedro Market, and enjoy a gentle walking tour around the magnificent Plaza de Armas.

4

Sacred Valley — Ruins & Markets

Sacred Valley

Descend into the Sacred Valley to explore the Pisac ruins perched high above the valley floor, browse the famous Pisac market, marvel at the fortress of Ollantaytambo, and visit the extraordinary circular Moray agricultural terraces.

5

Sacred Valley — Salt & Traditions

Urubamba

Visit the stunning Maras salt mines — thousands of evaporation pools cascading down the mountainside, in use since Inca times. Meet a local weaving community to learn about traditional Andean textiles and techniques. Overnight in the charming town of Urubamba.

6

Train to Aguas Calientes

Aguas Calientes

Board the scenic railway that winds along the Urubamba River through lush cloud forest to the gateway town of Aguas Calientes. Spend the afternoon exploring this mountain village and relaxing in the natural hot springs.

7

Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu

Rise before dawn for the magical sunrise entry to Machu Picchu. Your expert guide reveals the history and mysteries of the Lost City of the Incas. For the adventurous, an optional hike up Huayna Picchu offers breathtaking panoramic views. Return to Cusco by train in the afternoon.

8

Cusco — Ancient & Artisan

Cusco

Discover Cusco's rich heritage with visits to the imposing fortress of Sacsayhuamán, the Inca sun temple of Q'oricancha, and the city's artisan workshops where traditional crafts are still practised. The afternoon is yours to explore at your own pace.

9

Lake Titicaca & Puno

Puno / Lake Titicaca

Fly to Puno on the shores of Lake Titicaca, the world's highest navigable lake. Visit the remarkable floating Uros islands, handmade entirely from totora reeds, then continue to Taquile Island. An optional Amantání homestay offers an unforgettable overnight with a local family.

10

Departure

Juliaca

Transfer to Juliaca airport for your onward journey, or continue your adventure with an extension into Bolivia. Farewell, Peru — until next time.
