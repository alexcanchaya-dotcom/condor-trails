Your Journey

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Seven days of Caribbean bliss — from turquoise waters and island excursions to colonial history and jungle adventure.

1

Arrive Punta Cana

Punta Cana

Welcome to the Dominican Republic. Transfer to your all-inclusive beachfront resort in Punta Cana or Cap Cana. Settle in, take your first dip in the turquoise Caribbean, and enjoy a welcome cocktail as the sun sets over palm-fringed Bávaro Beach.

2

Resort Day

Punta Cana

Full day to enjoy your resort's beach, pools, water sports, and spa. Optional: catamaran cruise along the coast with snorkelling stops over coral reefs and a visit to the natural swimming pool (sandbar).

3

Saona Island

Saona Island

Full-day excursion to Saona Island — a protected nature reserve off the southeast coast. Boat through mangroves, swim in a natural pool with starfish, lunch on the beach, and return by speedboat through turquoise shallows.

4

Resort & Water Activities

Punta Cana

Morning at the resort. Afternoon: choose from kayaking, stand-up paddleboarding, snorkelling the house reef, or a sunset horseback ride along the beach.

5

Santo Domingo Day Trip

Santo Domingo

Drive to the capital (2 hrs). Explore the UNESCO Colonial Zone — the oldest European settlement in the Americas. Visit the Alcázar de Colón, Catedral Primada de América, and Calle Las Damas. Lunch in a colonial courtyard restaurant. Return to resort by evening.

6

Adventure Day

Punta Cana Region

Choose your adventure: 27 Waterfalls of Damajagua (jump, slide, and swim through 27 cascading waterfalls in the jungle), zip-lining through the tropical canopy, or a guided ATV tour through the countryside and local farms. Evening: farewell beach dinner.

7

Departure

Punta Cana

Final morning swim. Check out and transfer to Punta Cana airport. Farewell, Caribbean.
