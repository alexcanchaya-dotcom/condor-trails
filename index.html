Our Story

Why Condor Trails

We're not a booking platform. We're a team of local experts — born and raised across South America — who design every journey personally. With family roots in Peru and partnerships across the continent, we offer something no algorithm can: real knowledge, real connections, and real care.


Every guide we work with is chosen by hand. Every lodge we recommend, we've slept in. Every trail we suggest, we've walked. When you travel with Condor Trails, you're not booking a package — you're gaining a family on the other side of the world.

Local Expertise

Our team lives and breathes South America. Every guide, every lodge, every hidden trail — we know it firsthand.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees, no commission markups. Prices in USD, EUR, or GBP — what you see is what you pay.

Sustainable Travel

We partner with community-owned lodges, fair-wage guide programs, and carbon-offset initiatives across every route.

24/7 Support

Your dedicated trip concierge is available around the clock — before, during, and after your journey.
